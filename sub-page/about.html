About Us

Fundraising made simple, impactful and successful with Pointzon

"Welcome to Pointzon, the premier fundraising platform for startups and venture capitalists. Our mission is to connect innovative startups with the investors they need to grow and succeed.


We understand that fundraising can be a daunting task for startups, and finding the right investors can be challenging. That's why we've created a user-friendly platform that streamlines the process and makes it easy for startups to connect with potential investors. Our platform also allows investors to easily find startups that align with their investment goals.


Our team is made up of experienced entrepreneurs and investors who understand the challenges of fundraising and are passionate about helping startups succeed. We are dedicated to providing our users with the best possible experience and are constantly working to improve our platform.


We believe that startups are the driving force behind innovation and economic growth, and we are committed to helping them succeed. We are excited to be a part of your journey and are here to support you every step of the way.


Thank you for choosing Pointzon. Let's change the world together!"





Giving back to the Community

At Pointzon, we believe in the power of community and the impact that collective action can have on creating a better world. That's why we have dedicated a section of our platform to charity campaigns. We understand that not everyone can contribute financially, which is why we have made it easy for our users to donate to causes they care about directly on our platform.


However, we want to be transparent that while we do encourage charitable giving on our platform, we do not currently donate a portion of our profits to charity. Instead, we focus on providing a platform that empowers individuals and organizations to make a difference in their communities through fundraising and awareness-raising efforts. We believe that by providing a platform that makes it easy for individuals to give, we can have a greater impact than if we were to donate a portion of our profits.


We are constantly evaluating our approach to charity and looking for ways to make a positive impact in the world, and we appreciate the support of our users in making that happen.
